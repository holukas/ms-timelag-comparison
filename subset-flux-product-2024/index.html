Loads the final flux product FP2025.3 (CH-CHA, 2005–2024) from its external location, optionally keeps a chosen list of columns, extracts all half-hourly records for the year 2024, and saves the subset as Parquet under data/04-flux-product-2025.3_subset_2024/.

This 2024 subset provides the independent reference fluxes used alongside the time-lag processing versions.
